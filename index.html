Who we are

Built for Hartlepool.
Owned by the community.

HartlePower is a Community Interest Company — which means every penny we make above our costs goes back into Hartlepool. Not to shareholders. Not to executives. Back into the clubs, groups, charities and causes that make this town what it is.

When the Hartlepool Voluntary Development Agency closed in 2017 after 31 years, it left over 500 local groups without the infrastructure support they depended on. HartlePower stepped in — and hasn't stopped since.

About HartlePower

Profit for purpose. Space for community.

HartlePower is a Community Interest Company dedicated to providing affordable space and services across Hartlepool. When you rent from us, every pound above our costs goes back into local clubs, groups, and causes.

Based at the historic Greenbank estate — originally the home of the founder of Camerons Brewery — we've transformed a heritage building into a thriving hub for businesses and community organisations alike.
